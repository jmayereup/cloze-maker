You can use this program to generate fill-in-the-blank cloze exercises. Simply enter your text in the input field. Words that you want to remove should be surrounded by asterisks (*). Do not include punctuation within the asterisks or it may not render correctly.

  1. Enter your text and add asterisks around the words you want to remove.
  2. Click "Generate HTML."
  3. Click "Copy to Clipboard."
  4. Paste the code into an HTML block in your post.
